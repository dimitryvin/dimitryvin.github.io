A Little Wiser

A Little Wiser does one thing: it helps you go to bed a little wiser each night. One short, quiet session — then it tells you to come back tomorrow.

How the nightly session works

  • One idea in — a single mental model with a quote that illustrates it.
  • Reflect — what did you learn today, and where were you wrong?
  • Capture — one keepsake line: how are you wiser tonight?

Frequently asked

Why can’t I open the session right now?

The session unlocks during your chosen wind-down window — a three-hour stretch starting at the time you set. Outside that window you’ll see a gentle “come back later” screen. You can change your wind-down time in Settings.

How do I change my wind-down time or reminder?

Open Settings (the gear on the Today screen) and adjust the wind-down hour and the optional evening reminder.

Where are my entries stored? Will they sync?

Your almanac lives on your device and, if you’re signed in to iCloud, syncs privately across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac through your own iCloud account. There are no accounts to create.

I got a new device — how do I get my almanac back?

Sign in to the same iCloud account and open A Little Wiser. Your entries sync down automatically. Make sure iCloud Drive is enabled.
